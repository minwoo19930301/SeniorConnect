# Idea Backlog

The MVP remains Call, YouTube, Speak, and Camera. These ideas are ranked by impact, feasibility, and safety.

## Add immediately as cross-cutting controls

### Repeat Slowly — `MVP`

Replay the last guidance at a slower speech rate with shorter clauses. This is more universally useful than adding another feature button.

### Take Me Home — `MVP`

A permanent, large escape action. It should never require model reasoning.

## Strong next ideas

### Translate What I See — `FUTURE`

Part of Camera rather than another button. Read and translate signs, menus, transit notices, and short letters aloud.

### Suspicious Message Check — `FUTURE`

The user deliberately shares a screenshot or photo. The agent points out suspicious signals and recommends a safe next step, but never guarantees “safe” or “scam.” No links are opened automatically.

### Remember This — `FUTURE`

Store a simple user-authored note such as “The spare key is in the blue drawer.” Avoid medication schedules, appointments requiring clinical reliability, or inferred memory monitoring.

### Family Photo Story — `FUTURE`

Show approved family photos and read short family-written captions. This supports connection without surveillance or face recognition.

### Step-by-Step Task Cards — `FUTURE`

Family-approved instructions for a small household task, one step at a time, with Repeat and Stop. Do not use for medical devices or hazardous tasks.

### Managed Device Edition — `FUTURE`

For a carrier, clinic, or care organization: device-owner provisioning could enforce an app allowlist and installation restrictions. This is not a normal consumer Play Store feature.

## Attractive but wrong for this hackathon

### Automatic family discovery — `NON_GOAL`

Contact frequency and labels do not prove a relationship. Use explicit pairing.

### “Is this medicine safe?” — `NON_GOAL`

The camera can read visible label text but cannot diagnose, prescribe, or recommend dosage.

### Automatic scam blocking — `NON_GOAL`

False confidence is dangerous. Explain signals and escalate instead.

### Emergency dispatch — `NON_GOAL`

It requires location, identity, uptime, liability, and regional integrations beyond a three-day MVP.

### Remote family control — `NON_GOAL`

Family cannot watch the screen, use the microphone, or control the phone remotely.

### Universal phone automation — `NON_GOAL`

It is brittle, difficult to explain, and conflicts with the policy boundary that the user remains in control.

## Recommendation

Do not add a separate Search button. Keep search inside Speak. If one feature must be dropped, retain Camera and Call and replace cross-app YouTube guidance with user-triggered Explain a Screenshot.
